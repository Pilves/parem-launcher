<p><b>Are you using your phone, or is your phone using you?</b></p><p><i>Parem Launcher</i> is a text-only Android launcher: minimal on the surface, hyperfunctional underneath. No icons, no ads, no data collection.</p><p><b>THE OMNIBOX</b></p><p>The app drawer search bar is a single point of truth:</p><ul><li>Type — apps filter instantly, a single match auto-launches</li><li>Fuzzy match — "gm" finds Google Maps, "s ki" finds Shaurmas Kitchen</li><li>"2+2" — inline calculator, enter copies the result</li><li>Type a phone number — enter opens the dialer</li><li>Start with a space — enter searches Google; "!bang" queries go to DuckDuckGo</li></ul><p><b>DIGITAL WELLBEING</b></p><ul><li>Focus Mode: timed sessions that block all but 5 chosen apps (never your phone app)</li><li>Screen time on the home screen, 7-day usage graph, soft per-app time limits</li><li>Bad-habit apps ask "Open anyway?" once their daily limit is reached</li><li>Daily open counts next to each app — how often is more telling than how long</li></ul><p><b>EVERYTHING ELSE</b></p><ul><li>Up to 8 pinned apps, folders, quick notes, swipe-up companion apps</li><li>Multiple widgets with drag-to-resize</li><li>Gesture letters: draw a letter to launch an app</li><li>Dark/light themes on a schedule or by sunrise/sunset</li><li>Icon packs, weather (Open-Meteo, no account), daily wallpapers</li><li>Settings export/import as JSON</li></ul><p><b>Accessibility Service:</b> used exclusively for double-tap to lock the screen. Optional, disabled by default, collects nothing.</p><p>Free and open source under GPLv3. Based on Olauncher by tanujnotes.</p>
